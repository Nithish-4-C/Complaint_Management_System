About Our System

The Complaint Management System provides a streamlined platform for students to raise concerns about hostel, lab, or classroom issues. Administrators receive real-time visibility into all complaints and can efficiently prioritize, track, and resolve them — ensuring no issue goes unnoticed.

Built with modern web technologies, the system ensures a smooth experience for both users and admins, with a focus on transparency and accountability.
