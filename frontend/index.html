About IssueDesk

IssueDesk bridges the communication gap between users and administrators by providing a centralized platform for structured issue handling. Whether it's a hostel concern, a lab malfunction, or a classroom problem — every issue gets the attention it deserves.

With a focus on efficiency and transparency, IssueDesk ensures that every reported issue is tracked from submission to resolution, keeping both parties informed at every step.
